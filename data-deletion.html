User Data Deletion

If you have used Facebook Login in our app and would like your data to be deleted, you can request it at any time.

How to Request Data Deletion

Please send us an email at support@yourdomain.com with the subject line Facebook Data Deletion.

Once we receive your request, we will delete all data associated with your Facebook account from our systems within 48 hours.

Need Help?
